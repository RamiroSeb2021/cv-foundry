# Career Quality Checks

## CV and resume checks

- Facts preserved: employer, role, dates, tools, education, languages, URLs.
- Claims are interview-defensible; no invented metrics or seniority inflation.
- Bullets emphasize action, technical scope, method, and result when evidence exists.
- Technical skills are ordered by relevance to the target role, not alphabetically by default.
- ATS terms are natural and exact where truthful; no keyword stuffing.
- LaTeX syntax remains intact when editing `.tex` sections.

## Tailoring checks

- Job description keywords are mapped to real evidence in the CV.
- Summary/profile mirrors the target role without pretending a different background.
- Most relevant bullets appear first within each role.
- Added or rephrased claims are documented for interview defense.
- Gaps are flagged as gaps, not hidden with vague wording.

## Interview prep checks

- Each STAR story comes from a real CV bullet or user-provided experience.
- Stories cover leadership, problem solving, collaboration, delivery, and growth/failure when possible.
- Each story has a concise answer and a deeper follow-up version.
- The candidate can explain every technology, metric, and decision mentioned.
- Questions for the interviewer are specific to role success, team context, and expectations.
